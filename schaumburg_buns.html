Meet Our Schaumburg Buns

Woodfield Tech Center, 1119 Tower Road, Schaumburg IL 60173

Meet the bunnies of Cuddle Bunny Too, our boarding facility and appointment-only interaction space.

Bonded Pair

Midnight & Amor

A bonded pair of beautiful bunnies who will steal your heart. Amor is a Continental-Checkered Giant mix with immense energy who will crawl quickly into your lap for some attention and treats. Midnight is older, laid back, and likes to observe Amor's antics.

Solo Bun

Brownie

A Holland Lop with big, beautiful, brown eyes and chocolate brown, silky fur. She will bunny purr if you scratch her in just the right spot. She does not have a bonded partner and that is just fine by her—she likes all the attention and all the snacks!

Bonded Pair

Coco & Domino

An inseparable bonded pair. They are extremely active and it is enjoyable to watch them zoom around playing bunny tag. Coco is a Mini Lop, very food motivated, and quickly warms up, with shy guy Dutch-Polish mix Domino following closely behind.

Bonded Pair

David & Goliath

Two big, chill, Agouti colored Flemish Giant brothers who enjoy lounging and receiving pets. David is tinier and inquisitive. Goliath is massive with gigantic ears attached to an equally gigantic head. He likes to sit back and watch life pass him by.

Solo Bun

Furbie

A double-coated Lionhead with tan fur that feels like cotton candy. Always cautious, she rarely blinks and keeps a constant eye on visitors. She has bonded with Peter and happily remains in the background. Fun fact—Furbie & Lzzy are half sisters!

Bonded Pair

Lily & Petunia

A shy pair of inseparable Lop-mix siblings. Lily is the larger of the two loaves with a beautiful Chinchilla colored coat. Petunia has light gray fur with light tan splotches. They take a few minutes to warm up, but gaining their approval is well worth the wait.

Bonded Pair

Lzzy & Marley

As a Lop-Lionhead mix, Lzzy frequently has one ear standing straight up and the other tilted to the side. Her bonded partner Marley is a Holland Lop with shiny, soft black fur and tiny gray feet. He may have a few bald patches where Lzzy loves him too much!

Solo Bun

Loki

A fan-favorite Holland Lop. He is the sweetest bunny you could ever possibly meet. His soulful eyes will mesmerize you. He loves any and all attention, particularly neck scratches. He flops dramatically with his ears tossed askew and scares us until we realize he is still breathing!

Solo Bun

Peter

A gorgeous mini Rex rabbit with a cloud soft copper coat, charming cream belly, and cream eyeliner. His lively personality has blossomed since bonding with gal pal Furbie. He is confident and food motivated, which endears him to everyone he meets.
