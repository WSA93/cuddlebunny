Our Space

The Environment

A quiet, comfortable place where people can interact with bunnies and fellow humans, and where only peace and love are allowed to cross the threshold.

Cuddle Bunny features 3 spacious enclosures, with a bonded pair of rabbits in each one.

Pure Positivity

Positivity reigns! We ask visitors to leave any negativity at the door. Often in our society, activities are centered around food, drink, and mind-altering substances.

Cuddle Bunny is a place to find pure enjoyment through the simple oxytocin release experienced when petting a bunny. We ask visitors to arrive clear-minded and open to an alternative form of pure happiness and joy.

Cleanliness & Reality

We strive to maintain a clean space with air filtered by a central HEPA purification system. That said, visitors with FOFF (Fear of Floating Fur) and contact lens wearers should beware.

Brushes are available and visitors are encouraged to kick back and brush our furry friends. We are diligent about sweeping, but you may find a stray "cocoa puff" (chewed hay). If tiny brown balls aren't your thing, a visit probably isn't either.

Bunny Behavior

Bunnies are enjoyable simply by being bunnies. Literally everything they do is adorable. If you are expecting backflips and cartwheels, you may not find a visit enjoyable—and that is totally fine, Cuddle Bunny is not for everyone.

Bunnies can be aloof, requiring visitors to slowly, gently, and quietly initiate interactions at their ground level. If a bunny chins you or happens to give you a kiss, consider yourself extremely special!

Care & Free Roam

Our bunnies are incredibly social and thrive on human attention. We ensure their health in the least stressful environment possible. In addition to daily nap time from 1-3pm, bunnies alternate days upstairs—after a day of interacting, they have two days off.

A master calendar tracks when each bunny or bonded pair has an entire day to free-roam upstairs (approx. 3x per month), where they can greet visitors at their leisure or simply loaf behind the couch.

Diet & Health

Their diet consists of 80% Oxbow timothy hay, 10% fresh greens, 5% enriched pellets, and 5% treats (apples, bananas, blueberries). Water is sanitized and refreshed daily. Litter pans are lined, filled with hay, and changed daily.

They receive weekly papaya tablets and a Laxatone supplement for digestion. Nails are trimmed, and weight & temperature are taken monthly. Intake/output is monitored, they are regularly vetted, spayed/neutered, and fully vaccinated.

Evening Routine

At the end of the day while the humans are closing up, our bunnies are given free roam of the interaction area to stretch, exercise, and binky about. The carpeted runner spanning the length of the space is the bunny race track!

At night, the bunnies retreat to their spacious bunny condos downstairs filled with blankets, chew toys, and tunnels. They particularly enjoy hearing each other's antics and thrive on this consistent daily routine.

Ready to Step Into Peace?

Whether you seek an afternoon of quiet connection or the finest care for your companion, we are ready to welcome you.
