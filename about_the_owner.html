Meet The Founder

About Barbara Burdick

The goal of the owner is to share her love of bunnies with those who might not otherwise have access to these wonderful creatures. She was born into a dog-loving family and has had them as pets since birth including Beagles, a Basset, a Bichon, Dachshunds, a Chiweenie, a Black Labrador Retriever, a Newfoundland-mix, a Shih Tzu, and currently a Rottweiler, a Vizsla-mix, and a 25 year old box turtle. As a child, she had various pets including a duck, a crow, a parakeet, Monarch caterpillars, and freshwater fish.

In addition to being an all-around animal lover, she has also been an active and passionate community volunteer since high school. It is her belief that volunteering helps people connect and makes the world a better place. In a country with such tremendous wealth, no human or animal should ever be without the necessities of clean water, food or shelter.
