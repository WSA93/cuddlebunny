Resident Companions

Meet Our Lakeview Buns

2901 N Clark St, Chicago, IL 60657, United States

Meet the bunnies of Cuddle Bunny, our boarding facility and appointment-only interaction space.

Bonded Pair

Anna & Elsa

Anna is half of our pair of Frozen sisters. She is a black & white Dutch bunny who may be wide-eyed at first introduction, but quickly relaxes and flattens to the floor with a gently stroke to the tops of her shiny little black ears. The shape of a football upon arrival, her movement was very limited. After a healthy diet, she is now healthy and happy. The other half of our Frozen sisters, Elsa is a beautiful crisp, white Mini Rex with eyes as blue as the sky, pink inside her ears like cotton candy, and fur that feels like you're touching the air. When not nosily periscoping to see what's going on, she flops over sleeping soundly and snoring! She is by far our most vocal bunny.

Solo Bun

Cashmere

Although Cashmere (aka Cash) looks like a Siamese cat, we can assure you he is a Mini Rex bunny. In a softness competition, he has taken the prize. Cash absolutely loves treats and often climbs into visitors' laps for a crispy nibble. He also loves neck pets. After a few scratches to the back of his neck, he flattens into a bunny puddle on the floor and will stay there as long as the scratches continue. He just loves to be loved!

Bonded Pair

Fiona & Little Bear

Fiona is a wide-eyed Agouti colored Netherland Dwarf with an adorable white heart outlining her nostrils. She enjoys being scratched between her tiny rounded ears so much that, if you stop scratching, she will nudge your hand until you resume. She is a speed demon and loves to race and binky about! Lil Bear is a Holland Lop bunny and Fiona's bonded partner. He has a shiny, soft, medium length black coat and puffy gray feet. He is a mellow fellow and loyal husbun seldom seen away from her side.

Solo Bun

Fraggle

Fraggle, or Fraggle Floof as we like to call him, is an English Angora bunny who came to us matted from the tips of his ears to the tip of his tail. After much careful grooming, he has become a fluffy-eared, mutton-chopped beauty. With his gentle demeanor, unique appearance, and huge brown eyes, Fraggle has become a fan favorite. He's not into cilantro, but will gently nibble a treat from your fingers tips. He was born on Valentine's Day and is the biggest sweetheart you'll ever meet! Fun fact: Angoras are the sheep of the bunny world and must be regularly sheared. Fraggle's fur is collected and donated to local fibre arts students to create yarn! How cool is that?!?

Bonded Pair

Jacques & Sweetie

Jacques is a Champagne D'Argent bunny who was all black with a silver crescent moon on his side when he arrived. The silver slowly crept over his entire body leaving only his nose and eyes as dark as midnight. Sweetie arrived around the same time and they immediately became inseparable. It took some time for her to be named, so we started calling her Sweetie and it stuck because she is a total sweetie! While most Himalayan bunnies are black & white, Sweetie is a Lilac Himalayan due to the tan coloring of her ears, nose, paws and tail which is absolutely gorgeous coupled with her striking and expressive pink eyes.

Bonded Pair

Kirby & Waddle Dee

Waddle Dee and Kirby are tan Mini Lops with black noses and ears. Although they look the same, they are not related. Waddle is the larger of the two and Kirby has white markings on his left ear, which is how we tell them apart. They are as clever and cute as they are curious. No matter what's happening, Kirby just wants to be involved and Waddle is not far behind. Kirby is the king of splooting and Waddle is his shy but sweet gal pal who loves pets--just don't pet her behind as she's a little quirky and does not appreciate that. Both will dash quickly to your side at the sight of some crispy cilantro.

Solo Bun

Olaf

Olaf is a solo bunny with a big personality who tends to love the other bunnies just a little too much for their liking. We're told his fur feels like powdered sugar or a cloud--you be the judge! He has sparkling, beautiful, blue eyes that hint at the inner workings of his clever mind. Olaf has been trained to give high-fives, spin in circles, and stand up. He is highly food motivated and enjoys climbing into the laps of visitors for a snack. Or lay down and he will quickly hop onto your back for the best bunny massage ever!

Solo Bun

Pepe

Pepe is a black and white spotted Holland Lop who steals the hearts of absolutely everyone he meets. He loves food, yes, but even more so adores attention. And if he feels he is being ignored, he may become adorably demanding, crawling into your lap or giving you a gentle nudge to remind you of his fluffy presence. A chunk of his right ear is missing thanks to an aggressive former partner, but we think this only adds to his roguish charm. He is named after Pepe LePew because of his amorous nature and he has never met another bunny or a human he did not like.

Bonded Pair

Rex & Lola

Rexy is the boss bun and the OG of Cuddle Bunny. He is a chill strawberry blonde Continental Giant bunny weighing in at a hefty 13 lbs. He explores the world with his nose and has been known to stand on his hind legs to reach a treat, showing off just how tall and majestic he truly is! Lola is our smallest bunny and is bonded with Rex. While this tiny Netherland Dwarf-Lionhead mix looks like a devil with pointy black ears resembling horns and pink eyes, they are truly an angel. Lola is a supportive partner assisting Rex with his grooming as he ages.

Solo Bun

Winston

Winston is a big, doofy Flemish Giant baby and the largest of the Cuddle Bunny bunnies weighing in at a whopping 15 lbs. His ears are so large it appears a strong wind could easily tip him over. Winston loves to run and binky, but doesn't get much air with all that weight behind him. But it's sure fun to watch! While an imposing figure, he is the gentlest of souls, just as his Auntie GG was (RIP beautiful girl). We have to laugh when visitors at times hesitate to interact with him due to his size.
