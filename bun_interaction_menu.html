The Experience

What exactly does a bunny interaction entail?

Everyone must wash hands upon arrival. Visitors are provided a fluffy pillow and a sprig of cilantro in each enclosure to feed the bunnies. We wish we could give more cilantro, but they would have upset tummies by the end of the day! 15-20 minutes is spent in each enclosure with either a solo or bonded pair of buns.
