Gatherings & Celebrations

Private Events & Parties

From intimate gatherings to creative celebrations, make your next special occasion unforgettable with the company of our delightful bunnies.

Kids Birthday Parties

Celebrating a special occasion? We offer bunny-themed parties for kids at our Lakeview location with Bunny Crafts or Fun & Games like BUNGO (it's like BINGO, but cuter), Wrap the Mummy, Hot Bunny (like hot potato). You bring the cake, we've got the buns. Ages 8 and up.

Adult Birthday Parties

Bring your friends to our Lakeview location for a creative tie-dye or neon-blacklight painting party. "I Partied My Buns Off" t-shirts are available to dye or design with neon fabric markers. Kids Parties can be adapted for adults as well. Other party ideas? We're open to suggestions!

Tie-Dye Party

Choose from a selection of fun items to dye like t-shirts, sweats, scarves, totes, hats, bandanas, and more. Another dyeable option is our "I Partied My Buns Off" t-shirts! Primary as well as neon dye colors are available. Bring some snacks & some beverages and party your buns off!

Private Bunny Yoga & Pilates

Our amazing instructors will treat your group (minimum of 6 adults) to a special private class, with the help of a few super social bunnies. Due to space constraints, yoga is available only at our Lakeview location early morning or at day's end. Chair yoga is also available!

Guided Visualization

Chill out and find some inner peace with a unique guided visualization session. With bunnies, of course! You'll wear your most comfortable clothes, lay on a soft mat with feet propped & eyes closed while being transported to a tranquil alternate realm. Return relaxed and refreshed.

Craft Night

Gather your craftiest friends and get inspired by our bouncing bunnies. Choose between bunny crafts such as totes or terra cotta pots, collaging, faux cactus creation, etc. All materials are provided. You bring the snacks & friends!

Poetry & Creative Writing

A quiet night of guided self-expression might be exactly what you need to connect with your friends (minimum of 6) and reconnect with yourself. We provide the materials and bunnies; you bring the creativity!

Couples Connection

Enjoy a fun candlelit night out with friends (and bunnies!) playing our version of the Newlywed Game, reconnecting and slow dancing. Winning couple receives a kitschy gift! Flowers & chocolates included. Minimum of 3 couples required (max 6).

Other Ideas?

If you have other ideas for your perfect party that includes our bunnies, we're open to suggestions! As a small business, we're flexible and can help you bring your ideas to life!
