Sanctuary First.
Business Second.

Cuddle Bunny is a one-of-a-kind space where people can slow down, connect, and experience the gentle joy of being around rabbits. Whether you’re visiting for a calming daytime escape or a cozy weekend activity, our goal is simple: help you relax, recharge, and feel good.

We combine animal interaction with wellness and creativity. From bunny yoga and meditation to art nights, crafting, and quiet reading sessions, every experience is designed to nurture your mind, body, and spirit in a warm, welcoming environment.

At the heart of Cuddle Bunny are our rescued rabbits, each one deeply loved, carefully cared for, and an important part of our family. Their wellbeing is our highest priority, and we’ve built our space and practices around ensuring their comfort and safety.

Beyond our studio, we’re committed to making a positive impact through community service, education, and meaningful partnerships. Whether it’s creating moments of comfort, encouraging responsible pet care, or bringing people together, we believe small, gentle experiences can make a big difference.

Come for the bunnies. Stay for the peace, connection, and joy.

Rabbit Boarding Services

We provide limited, high-quality boarding for rabbits requiring short or extended stays. Leave with absolute peace of mind knowing your furry family member is receiving unparalleled, individualized care.

  • HEPA-filtered facility ensuring optimal air quality
  • Spacious, carpeted exercise pens for ultimate comfort
  • Individual care with no interaction between boarded guests
  • Regular updates for pet parents to stay connected
  • High standards of cleanliness maintained at all times

More Than A Sanctuary

The Cuddle Bunny Pillars

A holistic approach to wellness, community connection, and uncompromising animal welfare.

1. Wellness & Holistic Activities

We offer activities that nurture the body, mind, and spirit. Evening sessions typically run 6–8 p.m. on weekends.

  • Bunny yoga & meditation
  • Sketching, knitting, and crocheting
  • Book reading, poetry & creative writing
  • Crafting, tie-dye, and neon blacklight painting

2. Community Engagement

We believe in creating a positive social impact far beyond our sanctuary doors. We proudly host community service events like food drives and coordinate volunteering efforts to support the food-insecure. By working hand-in-hand with local organizations, we strive to uplift our neighborhood and build a stronger, more compassionate community.

3. Animal Rescue & Care

We focus on high-quality, loving care for all resident rabbits, rescuing most of our bunnies from difficult situations or surrendered homes.

  • All resident bunnies are spayed/neutered
  • Bi-annually vaccinated (RHDV2)
  • Not available for adoption or sale

4. Retail

Take a piece of The Cuddle Bunny with you. Our boutique retail space sells a curated selection of premium bunny-themed products.

  • Exclusive apparel, jewelry, and accessories
  • Stickers, buttons, and enamel pins
  • Artisan mugs and soft plush toys
